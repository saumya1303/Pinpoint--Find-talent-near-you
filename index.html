about us

what makes pinpoint special?

The world is filled with talent, people find themselves being good at the most absurd things - from being good at pottery to being good at nail art. Quite frequently, however, people do not pursue what they are best at - because of a lack of economic sustenance, arising from a general lack of reach of clients in that particular field.

This is one target goal we at Pinpoint aim to fix - at Pinpoint we provide a portal accessible to everyone to advertise themselves in however fields they want. We nurture an audience of customers and showcase different service providers onto our platform for them to choose from, so in case somebody needs an urgent nail art, they are one call away from someone who almost never picked that profession, to begin with.
